About TMS

Talus Motorsports acquires and renovates vintage cars and 4×4 trucks for its own account (not a service available to third party customers). The vehicles are highly modified with upgraded parts, and they are typically designed for specialized applications, including track and off-road events. The company employs a team of engineers and mechanics across two facility locations in Georgia and New York.
